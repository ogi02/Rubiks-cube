PLL

Permutation of Last Layer (PLL) is the last step of the CFOP, and is the final straight in speedsolving the Rubik's cube. There are 21 different variations of PLL, and a well-known name for each. Therefore are required 21 algorithms to make a PLL solving in just 1 fast algorithm.
