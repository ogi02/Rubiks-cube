CMLL

CMLL is the third step of Roux, and it orients and permutes the corners simultaneously. This is an algorithmic step and there are 42 different algorithms. Since I am not speedsolving with the Roux method, I am going to show 2-Look CMLL, which is far easier and involves less algorithms.

Corner Orientation

The first step of 2-Look CMLL involves 7 algorithms with which all U layer corners will be oriented correctly.

Corner Permutation

The second step of 2-Look CMLL involves 2 algorithms with which all U layer corners will be permuterd correctly.
